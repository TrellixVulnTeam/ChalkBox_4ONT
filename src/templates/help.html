What is ChalkBox?

ChalkBox is a Learning Management System

What can you do with ChalkBox?

STUDENT

To view your registered courses click here

To view the syllabus click on the Syllabus link on top left corner of your course page

To view the new assignments and/or to submit assignments, click on assignmnets within the course page

To view Grades of a particular course, click on grades

INSTRUCTOR

To view your courses click here

To view the syllabus of your courses and/or to upload a new syllabus, click on the Syllabus link on top left corner of the course page

To upload a new assignments and/or to modify existing assignments, click on the assignment link in your course page

To view the submissions of each student, click on submissions

To view the class performance, click on grades
